20250515 Threaded holes
https://community.sw.siemens.com/s/question/0D54O00006kKdEySAK/getting-hole-type-from-hole-geometry
I recently dealt with this.

You must check not only .holetype, but also .treatmenttype

For example:
.holetype=33 is a normal hole
.holetype=34 is counterbore
.holetype=35 is countersink

Treatmenttype tells you if it is threaded or not.
.Treatmenttype=37 is normal threaded
.threaddescription tell you the type of thread (M8, M8 x 1, etc..)
